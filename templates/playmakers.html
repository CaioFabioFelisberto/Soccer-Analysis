The Playmakers' DNA

Progressive passes and progressive carries reveal two complementary ways to break a defensive block. This module separates pass-led controllers from dribble-led disruptors.

Top right quadrant: complete engines who influence progression in every phase.

Right side: maestros who manipulate tempo with passing volume.

Upper side: infiltrators who attack space through carrying and acceleration.

  • Use the right-hand side to find players who advance the ball with range and consistency.
  • Use the upper band to target ball-carrying specialists that damage compact blocks.
  • Use the overlap zone when scouting players that fit hybrid possession systems.

Insight: If a team dominates possession but struggles to create separation, the solution is often a profile higher on the Y-axis, not simply another passer.
