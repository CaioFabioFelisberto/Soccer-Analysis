Reality check

This module compares actual goals with expected goals to detect who is overperforming, underperforming, or converting at a sustainable elite level.

How to read it: players above the balance line are outperforming xG, while players below it are leaving goals on the table.

Scouting value: repeated overperformance may signal high finishing quality, but extreme spikes can also indicate unsustainable variance.

  • Track players close to xG as stable conversion profiles.
  • Flag large overperformers for deeper video review before projection.
  • Use underperformers to find buy-low opportunities with strong chance volume.

Insight: the best recruitment decisions come from balancing output, chance quality, and repeatability.
