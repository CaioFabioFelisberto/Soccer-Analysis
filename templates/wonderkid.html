Youth scouting

Hunting wonderkids

Players 21 or younger are filtered by per-90 efficiency so the chart highlights production, not just accumulated minutes.

Why it works: elite recruitment buys trajectory, not just current reputation.

What to watch: players who already show veteran-level output in shot creation or finishing efficiency.

  • Prioritize the upper-right quadrant for high-upside attackers with immediate impact signals.
  • Compare production per 90 instead of raw totals to avoid minute bias.
  • Use the minimum-playing-time filter as a first step, not the final verdict.

Insight: the best opportunities often appear before the market fully adjusts to the player's level.
